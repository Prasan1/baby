After Vaccination Care
What to Expect:
  • Soreness: At injection site for 1-2 days
  • Fever: Low-grade fever is normal
  • Fussiness: May be more irritable for a day
  • Drowsiness: May sleep more than usual
How to Help:
  • Give extra cuddles and comfort
  • Offer breast milk or formula more frequently
  • Cool compress on injection site if swollen
  • Dress baby in light clothing if feverish
When to Call Your Doctor
Call immediately if:
  • High fever (over 105°F/40.5°C)
  • Signs of severe allergic reaction
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Excessive, inconsolable crying for 3+ hours
  • Seizure or convulsions
Other reasons to call:
  • Fever lasting more than 2 days
  • Injection site very red, hot, or hard
  • Unusual behavior lasting more than 2 days
  • Any concerns about baby's reaction
